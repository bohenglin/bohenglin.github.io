Navigation Guide

Welcome to my page. Here contains my complete CV as well as some of the projects that I feel are important, either as milestones during my academic progress, or show my ability in certain area. Here are steps to navigate about:

1.1) The Menu bar is located on the left side of the page;

1.2) If you are visiting on a mobile device, click the Menue icon on the top left hand corner to bring out the Menu bar;

2.1) Click Home to return to main home page anytime;

2.2) Click Resume &CV to open my complete CV in this window (.pdf file);

2.3) Click Category to see collections of my work - Analysis Projects contains projects done using different programming languages, Data Visualizations contains projects done using Tableau and published on Tableau Public;

2.4) Click Contact Me to see ways to reach me, or send me an email (will prompt a new window);

2.5) Click About to read about me and my life so far :)

3) If you wish to connect me on other platforms such as LinkedIn, GitHub or Facebook, click on the respectively options on the bottom left.
